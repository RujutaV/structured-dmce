Campus Amenities

The college has a beautiful campus with various amenities and hi-tech infrastructure keeping in mind the educational and social growth of the students. Located in a serene atmosphere, the campus is spread over 2.47 acres of land. The institute is strategically located in the developed node of Airoli, Navi Mumbai, thoroughly connected to Mumbai- Thane and all other nodes of Navi Mumbai by bus and train.

Library

The fully computerized Central Library is located in 800 sq.m area. The library possesses more than 40000 technical books, magazines, national, international and e-journals from INDEST-AICTE Consortium. It has well trained staff to provide congenial atmosphere for the students, especially in locating and issuing books and also encourage them to avail free Internet facility. The facilities include an Access System, computerized bar coding system to issue and return the books, audio/video sets to view technical videos. Institutional membership of Indian Institute of Technology, Mumbai DELNET are also provided to students. This system enables the students to use it to the fullest.

Seminar Hall

To its credit the institute has an air conditioned seminar hall with a seating capacity of 200 members. Video conferences, orientation programs, seminars and many more are conducted smoothly here. The college keeps on inviting from time to time renowned academicians and intellectuals in different spheres for addressing the students and the teaching staff on various subjects, topics of academic and other co-related pursuits with a view to widen the horizon of knowledge of the students and the staff as well. Competitive programmes in difference academic spheres are also organized to give an exposure to their talents, potentialities and overall development of the students.

Canteen

The canteen offers abundant scope for the students to break free from their regular, hectic schedules and relish hygienic and appetizing eateries and beverages at subsidized prices.College canteen is one of the most happening places in the campus. Of course, it is known for its savory food, but it is also the place where you make some of the best memories of your college life.

Badminton Court

The badminton court provides students a huge amount of relaxation from their studies.

Language Lab

The Language Lab is an audio-visual installation with ETNL Software used as an aid to teach English in all the skills-listening, speaking, reading and writing. Students are free to avail this facility all the four years.Good communication skills are indispensable for the success in any profession. The language Laboratory facilitates individual tutoring by the staff members as well as self learning by the students. It helps students to acquire proficiency in the English language. Special emphasis is given to weaker/ Vernacular students to perform better in campus placements.
