President's Message







Mrs. Gauri Bhattacharya

President
Nagar Yuwak Shikshan Sanstha Airoli

Science and Technology has always been an integral part of Indian culture. The innate ability to perform creatively in science came to be backed with an institutional setup and strong support by many educationalists. In the recent years the contribution of these institutions has created tremendous opportunity for the nation to leapfrog to the next wave of technology. in research and public service. We are devoted to the conviction of strengthening human capacities towards the growth in the fields of Engineering and Technology. The innovative practices adopted are the steps towards imparting preeminent educational experiences that are responsive to the needs of 21st century. Our legacy is to value academic excellence, professionalism, ethics and cultural diversity among its faculty, staff and students.So I invite all the stakeholders to have single-minded devotion to achieve success.
