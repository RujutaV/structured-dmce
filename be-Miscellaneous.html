Miscellaneous

The medium of instruction for degree course in Engineering and Technology is English.
The Admission of all the students other than those who have appeared in Maharashtra State Board of Higher Secondary Examination to any course at the college will be confirmed only on production of the Eligibility Certificate from Mumbai University.
Physical fitness : The candidate shall furnish physical fitness certificate form Medical Authority along with the application form.

Conduct and Discipline:
If any of the statements made in application form or any information supplied by the candidate in connection with his or her admission is later on, at any time, found to be false or incorrect, his or her admission will be cancelled, fees forfeited and he or she may be expelled from the college by the Principal.

Undertaking:
All candidates who have applied for admission, shall be deemed to submit an undertaking enclosed herewith.

Cancellation of Admission
Cancellation of Admission is as per DTE Rules
