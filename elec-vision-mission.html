Vision

V1. To foster and percolate higher end education with value added engineering, technology programs providing all facilities in terms of technology and platform for development with social awareness.
V2. Graduating students who will be well educated with technical knowledge, with solid communication and team work skill, who can compete successfully in global business world and contribute significantly to the national base of engineering education and technology.
V3. To create a department where we can imbibe curiosity to students, who wish to become researchers and innovativeness and to students who wish to become technologist.

Mission

M1. To create the brand of institute which will be recognized globally.
M2. We nurture intellectual feasibility by integrating research, teaching and assessment to promote continuous improvement of our scholarly community.
M3. The department is committed to mobilize the resources and equip itself, faculty and materials by value additions.
