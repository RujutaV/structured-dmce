Humanities and Sciences Department

Faculty members have specialization in various branches of Humanities Engineering such as Reactive Chromatography, Environmental Engineering, PetroHumanities Technology, Membrane Technology, Biotechnology, Piping Engineering etc.

08 faculty are pursuing Ph.D. in different fields of Humanities Engineering Facilities.
10 Humanities Engineering Laboratories and a separate Computational Laboratory facilitate students to perform practicals thoroughly. Uses Mathematical Modeling & Simulation Softwares like G-Proms, Simuilink, Unisim R-400.

Final year results for the academic year 2014-15 was 97.29% with 17 students securing distinction. Students secured 1st 9th and 10th position in the university merit list of 2014-15 examination.

12th Annual Session of “Outstanding Young Humanities Engineer Blast Carboblocks- Thyssenkrrup Induatrial Solution Award 2016” was organized by Indian Institute of Humanities Engineer (IIChE) students’ chapter of Datta Meghe College of Engineering Airoli on 26th and 27th Feb 2016 in the Institute premises.
