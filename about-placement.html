As a part of the efforts to develop and strengthen the relationship with the corporate world, the college has delineated long term strategy to place Engineering students in prestigious organizations. The requisite inputs, the ongoing interaction with industry experts, and the large alumni enhance their practical knowledge. To say, the students are nurtured in the atmosphere of everyday learning. We also enhance the industry-institute interaction through various activities. Training and Placement cell encourages students to participate in various events sponsored by Industries. Consecutively for the last three years students from the college became Google student representative

    Training and Placement Cell Activities:


  • The Cell works round the year effectively and efficiently to liaison with major organizations .
  • Facilitate training, arranging for live projects and providing placement assistance to its students.
  • It also ensures an active interaction with the industry by organizing guest lectures and seminars.
  • Invites trainers in behavioural skills to enhance the soft skills of the Students.
  • Arranges for personality development sessions, career development programs.
  • Interview technique program to facilitate students to perform better in the campus recruitment program.
  • The Cell has organized various technical, non-technical Programs for the staff in order to develop the necessary skills, based on the suggestions from head of the Departments.
