Vision

To produce the employable graduates by providing Chemical Engineering knowledge for the welfare of the society.

Mission

M1: Impart knowledge and understanding of the Chemical Engineering Science through curriculum.
M2: Develop skills to become technically competent professionals.
M3: Inculcate social and life-long learning awareness.
